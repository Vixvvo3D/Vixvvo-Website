About Us

Message From The Owner

Andrew Eckhoff
Owner
Salt Lake City, Utah

My journey began at the age of 15 when I used my first paycheck from my job at the local hardware store to buy a Canon Elura 100 – my first camera that sparked an unwavering pursuit of perfection in visual storytelling. Along the way, I’ve been eager to learn, making many mistakes and growing from them while consistently seeking knowledge to refine my craft.

Today, we proudly continue to uphold these core values, transforming every project into a captivating visual experience.

When you choose Vixvvo, you can trust that the same enthusiasm and dedication that once drove a 15-year-old with a camera continues to inspire our work today. Each day, I am filled with gratitude for the opportunity to do what I love, and I never take this incredible privilege for granted. My hope is that this passion shines through in every project we undertake.

Thank you for considering Vixvvo. We look forward to crafting unforgettable stories together.
